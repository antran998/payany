RESERVE FUND

1. What is POC Protocol

POC (Proof Of Contribution) - This is a mining token algorithm based on the "Certificate of Dedication" when locking ETH into "reserve fund" value for DPA token.

2. How to get ETH out of Reserve Fund?

The only way to get the ETH out of the Reserve Fund is to swap the DPA token into the Smart Contract, which means that it will eliminate the number of DPAs that are circulating on the market.
